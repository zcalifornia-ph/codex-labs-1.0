# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all participants and contributors of CODEX LABS 1.0.

We expect everyone in this repository and related project spaces to:

- communicate respectfully and professionally
- discuss ideas and code, not personal attacks
- collaborate in good faith and welcome different perspectives
- share feedback that is constructive, specific, and actionable

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not allowed:

- harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or intimidation
- personal attacks, bullying, or sustained disruption
- sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- deliberate misrepresentation, plagiarism, or deception

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and workshop coordination channels where participation is connected to this repository.

## Reporting

To report violations or concerns, contact:

- Maintainer: Zildjian E. California
- Email: zecalifornia@up.edu.ph

Please include links, screenshots, logs, and relevant context when possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take actions necessary to protect community safety and project integrity, including:

- warnings
- content moderation or removal
- temporary restrictions
- permanent bans

Enforcement decisions will prioritize fairness, proportionality, and protection of participants.
